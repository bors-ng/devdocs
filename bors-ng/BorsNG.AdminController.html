    Bors-NG v0.1.0 BorsNG.AdminController View Source

    Functionality that is specific to administrators.

    Administrators are users with the is_admin flag set. Ensuring non-administrators can’t get here is done in BorsNG.Router.

    Administrators have the ability to step outside the permissions and edit any repo, or delete it if they wish.
